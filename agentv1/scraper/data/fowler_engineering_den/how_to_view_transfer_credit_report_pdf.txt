How to View your Transfer Credit Report 1. Log in to your student center. You can access it by clicking on the pawprint on Chapman's main webpage and clicking student center.
2. Once in your student center, select the Academic Records icon.
 3. Once in Academic Records, select Transfer Credit Report.
4. If you took college-level coursework, this information will be displayed under Course Credits. If you received AP/IB credit, your exams and the scores will be displayed under Test Credits. In this example, you can see that the student earned a five on their AP Calculus exam, which gave them credit for MATH 110 and satisfied their Quantitative GE. They also earned a five on their AP French exam, which gave them credit for French 102 and 201, which satisfied their language GE.
